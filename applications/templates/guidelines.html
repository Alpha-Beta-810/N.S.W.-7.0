Guidelines

(A) Project / Thesis

Students are highly encouraged to interact with CSIR-4PI Scientists and identify a guide and area of work prior to the formal submission of their application to SPARK. Research interests and e-mail addresses of CSIR-4PI scientists are given at www.csir4pi.in.

It is mandatory that students must have a faculty member of their University/Institute as a guide/co-guide. The name, designation, e-mail address and telephone number of the guide from their Institute must be provided while submitting the online application.

Please ensure that your application is submitted at least four weeks prior to the commencement of your Project/Thesis work. We will not be able to process late applications.

Online applications will be scrutinized by an expert committee at regular intervals. Students may be called for a discussion with the expert committee prior to their selection on a need basis. Selected students will be intimated through e-mail.

Selected students are requested to report to CSIR-4PI office on the project starting date (or the previous day) with the following documents:

  1. Originals of all mark cards and certificates starting from 10th onwards
  2. One xerox copy of all the originals
  3. Two copies of passport size photographs
  4. A certificate in the prescribed format (available for download) signed by the candidate, the internal guide from the University/Institute and the head of the department

Students reporting without the above documents will not be allowed to join CSIR-4PI.

Upon completion of the project, students will have to prepare a thesis for submission to their University/Institute and CSIR-4PI.

Currently, CSIR-4PI is not in a position to offer any stipend or provide accommodation to students.
(B) Internship

Maximum duration of Internship is 3 Months. Those students who need a signed Project/Thesis report should apply under "Project/Thesis" category.

The student may identify a CSIR-4PI scientist, who is willing to guide him/her during the internship. Please write to the Scientist and obtain e-mail consent from him. Also, discuss and agree upon the start date and duration with the guide.

Once the guide's consent is obtained, the student may submit his/her internship application at this portal. Please ensure that your application is submitted at least four weeks prior to the commencement of your internship.

Selected students are requested to report to CSIR-4PI office on the internship starting date with the following documents:

  1. Originals of all mark cards and certificates starting from 10th onwards
  2. One xerox copy of all the originals
  3. Two copies of passport size photographs
  4. A certificate in the prescribed format (available for download) signed by the candidate and the head of the department
Currently, CSIR-4PI is not in a position to offer any stipend or provide accommodation to summer internship candidates.
